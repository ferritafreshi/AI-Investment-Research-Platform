# AI Investment Research Platform

**An AI-powered local investment research platform that helps long-term investors discover, evaluate, and understand high-quality growth companies through explainable, evidence-based analysis.**

The AI Investment Research Platform is a personal project that combines artificial intelligence, financial analysis, and modern software development into a single local application. Its purpose is to streamline the investment research process by bringing together company financials, SEC filings, annual reports, earnings summaries, news, and other relevant information into an intuitive dashboard that explains not only *what* is happening, but *why* it matters. Built with a focus on long-term growth investing, the platform is designed to help its user make more informed, evidence-based investment decisions while serving as a hands-on journey to becoming an AI Product Builder.

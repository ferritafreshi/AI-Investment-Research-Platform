# AI Investment Research Platform

## Purpose

The AI Investment Research Platform exists to help me become a better long-term investor by combining disciplined investment research with the power of artificial intelligence.

Today, researching companies is scattered across dozens of websites, SEC filings, earnings-call transcripts, news articles, financial statements, and community discussions. It is time-consuming, difficult to organize, and easy to overlook important information. I also find it challenging to interpret financial reports and determine which information truly matters.

Rather than relying on opinions, headlines, or emotions, I want a single platform that gathers evidence, organizes it clearly, explains it in plain English, and helps me make more informed investment decisions.

This project is also my path to becoming an AI Product Builder. Instead of simply learning programming, I will learn how to design products, work effectively with AI coding assistants, organize software projects, and build practical applications that solve real problems.

## Who This Platform Is For

This platform is designed for one user: me.

I am a self-directed, long-term growth investor who wants to build a concentrated portfolio of outstanding businesses alongside low-cost index funds. My focus is on companies with durable competitive advantages, strong financial performance, healthy balance sheets, and long runways for future growth.

The platform should simplify complex financial information without oversimplifying it. It should teach me as I use it, helping me become increasingly confident in reading financial statements, evaluating businesses, and making investment decisions based on evidence rather than speculation.

## What Success Looks Like in 12 Weeks

By the end of the first 12 weeks, I will have a working Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that runs locally on my computer and serves as the foundation for a much larger investment research platform.

The MVP will allow me to:

* View a ranked list of approximately 10-20 high-quality U.S. growth companies.
* Display key financial metrics and company health indicators.
* Show transparent, explainable scoring based on financial quality, growth, valuation, and risk.
* Present AI-generated summaries of earnings, SEC filings, annual reports, and relevant news when available.
* Explain financial concepts and scores in clear, understandable language.
* Provide a clean, modern HTML dashboard that is easy to navigate and enjoyable to use.
* Maintain professional documentation and version control through GitHub.

Equally important, I will have developed a repeatable workflow for building AI-powered applications. I will understand how to plan a product, organize documentation, collaborate effectively with AI assistants, use GitHub confidently, and improve software through small, consistent iterations.

## Guiding Principles

Throughout this project, I will follow these principles:

* Evidence before opinions.
* Long-term investing over short-term speculation.
* Explainability over unnecessary complexity.
* Continuous learning through building.
* Small weekly improvements that produce visible progress.
* Professional documentation from the very beginning.
* Modular design that supports future expansion.
* AI as a collaborative partner, with important conclusions verified through reliable data and sound judgment.

## Long-Term Vision

This MVP is only the beginning.

Over time, the AI Investment Research Platform will evolve into my personal investment research assistant. It will help me monitor companies, compare opportunities, identify risks, understand changing business fundamentals, and continuously improve my investment process.

More importantly, this project will establish the foundation for future AI products. By completing it, I will have built not only an application I genuinely use, but also the skills, workflow, and confidence to create many more AI-powered tools in the years ahead.

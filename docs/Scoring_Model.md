# Scoring Model

## Overview
Every company receives six category scores from 0–100.
Each score includes a plain-English explanation and the evidence behind it.

## Categories

### 1. Revenue Growth (0–100)
Measures how fast the company is growing its top-line revenue.
- 90–100: 5-year CAGR above 20%
- 70–89:  5-year CAGR 12–20%
- 50–69:  5-year CAGR 7–12%
- 30–49:  5-year CAGR 3–7%
- 0–29:   5-year CAGR below 3% or negative

### 2. Financial Strength (0–100)
Measures balance sheet health and ability to service debt.
- Interest Coverage Ratio above 15x = excellent
- Interest Coverage Ratio 8–15x = good
- Interest Coverage Ratio 3–8x = acceptable
- Interest Coverage Ratio below 3x = risky
- Free Cash Flow positive and growing = bonus points
